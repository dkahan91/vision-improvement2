    How the programme works

    The eye exercises are arranged in 3 phases. The programme tells you when one phase is finished so that you can go onto the next somewhat harder one

    You can go through the phases as quickly or as slowly as you want, but remember somewhere along the line be prepared to find some exercise or other that is going to be difficult. This is exactly where your eyes inherent problem is showing up! But this is good news. Once we've found the trouble spot when we work at getting over it the cure is in sight.
